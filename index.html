Bio

Hiyo! I'm a Seattle-native recently returned home after traveling the globe with my family. I graduated from UW in 2009 and began teaching science for a nonprofit, joined the airforce as a korean analyst, and met my husband. We traveled across the country, started our family in Florida, and then moved across the world, adding to our family in Okinawa. At that point, I hung up the uniform in exchange for raising our two brilliant, beautiful girls. It's been almost 5 years, now, and as my littlest gears up to enter kindergarten, I am gearing up to restart my career!
